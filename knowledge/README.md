# Knowledge

## Purpose

The Knowledge domain contains assets that extract, organize, and preserve engineering knowledge from engineering activities.

---

## Responsibilities

- Convert engineering information into reusable knowledge assets.
- Support repository mining and architecture summarization.
- Capture incident learnings as reusable insights.
- Enable impact analysis across the engineering knowledge graph.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Engineering documentation procedures.
- Repository mining procedures.
- Incident learning frameworks.
- Architecture summary templates and procedures.
- Impact analysis procedures.
- Knowledge extraction Skills.

---

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Project-specific documentation (that belongs to the project).
- ADRs (those belong to `foundation/adr/`).
- Engineering capabilities like Skills and Templates (those belong to their respective domains).
- Customer knowledge.

---

## Knowledge is a Transversal Domain

Knowledge assets may reference and work with assets from any layer of the dependency hierarchy.

They extract and preserve information rather than define or execute engineering procedures.
