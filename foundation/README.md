# Foundation

## Purpose

The Foundation domain governs the Engineering Toolkit itself.

It contains the documents that define how the toolkit is structured, how it evolves, and how decisions are made.

---

## Responsibilities

- Define the constitutional architecture through Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).
- Document the vision and principles of the Engineering Toolkit.
- Establish governance policies and contribution guidelines.
- Preserve the architectural history of the toolkit.
- Record version history and changelog.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).
- Governance policies that apply to the toolkit itself.
- Version history and release notes.
- Contribution guidelines.
- Licensing information.

---

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Project-specific content.
- Customer information.
- Engineering assets intended for reuse (those belong to their respective domains).
- Context Bundles (those belong to consuming projects).
- Implementation code.

---

| Folder | Contents |
