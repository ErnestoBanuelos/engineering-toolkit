# Governance

## Purpose

The Governance folder contains policies, records, and management artifacts that apply to the Engineering Toolkit repository itself.

---

## Responsibilities

- Record approval decisions for Engineering Assets.
- Maintain the governance policy documents referenced by ADR-007.
- Track ownership assignments for published Engineering Assets.
- Store governance records that must remain discoverable over time.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Approval records for architectural changes.
- Governance policy documents.
- Ownership assignment records.
- Deprecation notices.
- Release governance records.

---

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- ADRs (those belong in `adr/`).
- Engineering assets (Skills, Templates, Standards, etc.).
- Project-specific governance.
- Context Bundles.

---

## Related ADRs

- ADR-007 — Repository Governance
- ADR-002 — Engineering Asset Model (lifecycle definitions)
- ADR-003 — Asset Metadata Model (ownership fields)
