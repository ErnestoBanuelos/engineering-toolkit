# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Engineering Toolkit is a professional engineering framework.

All contributors are expected to maintain engineering quality, intellectual honesty, and respectful collaboration.

---

## Engineering Standards

Contributors are expected to:

- Prioritize technical correctness over consensus.
- Challenge assumptions with evidence, not opinion.
- Accept independent review as a quality mechanism, not a personal evaluation.
- Follow the governance process defined in ADR-007.
- Preserve human accountability at all stages of engineering work.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following practices are not acceptable in this repository:

- Bypassing governance processes.
- Publishing assets without independent review.
- Allowing AI-generated content to pass as human-approved decisions.
- Introducing project-specific or confidential information into reusable assets.
- Modifying the constitutional architecture (ADR-000) without explicit human approval.

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct are handled through the governance process defined in ADR-007.

Architectural violations require a Level 4 review.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all contributions, reviews, and discussions within this repository.
